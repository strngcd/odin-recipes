Classic Spaghetti Carbonara (Dinner)

A creamy, egg-based Roman pasta dish without cream

Ingredients

  • 4 oz (120g) spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 oz (30g) Pecorino Romano or Parmesan, finely grated
  • 3 oz (90g) pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled (optional)
  • Black pepper, freshly cracked

Instructions

  1. Boil salted water and cook spaghetti until al dente.
  2. In a bowl, whisk eggs, grated cheese, and a generous amount of black pepper.
  3. In a pan, cook pancetta until crispy. Optional: add garlic cloves for flavor, then remove them.
  4. Reserve ½ cup pasta water, then drain spaghetti. Turn off the pancetta pan's heat, add spaghetti, and toss.
  5. Quickly pour in the egg mixture and toss vigorously. The residual heat will create a creamy sauce (add a splash of pasta water if too thick).
  6. Serve immediately with extra pepper and cheese

Enjoy your cooking!
