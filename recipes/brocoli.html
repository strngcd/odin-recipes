15-Minute Beef & Broccoli Stir-fry (Quick Dinner)

Faster and healthier than takeout

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) flank steak, thinly sliced against the grain
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce (or tamari for GF)
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water (slurry)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, ginger, and ½ cup water.
  2. Heat oil in a wok or large pan over high heat. Add beef and stir-fry for 2 minutes until browned. Remove beef.
  3. Add broccoli to the pan and stir-fry for 1 minute. Add 2 tbsp water, cover, and steam for 1 minute.
  4. Pour the sauce into the pan and bring to a simmer. Stir in the cornstarch slurry and cook until thickened (30 seconds).
  5. Return beef to the pan, toss to coat, and serve immediately over rice
